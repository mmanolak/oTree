Welcome to the Dictator Game.

In each round, you are randomly paired with another anonymous participant.

One of you is randomly chosen as the Dictator, the other as the Recipient.

  • The Dictator receives 10 points.
  • The Dictator decides how many of these 10 points to give to the Recipient.
  • The Recipient makes no decision in this game.

Payoffs in each round are:

  • The Dictator earns 10 minus the amount given.
  • The Recipient earns the amount given.

You will play this game for 3 rounds. In each round you are re-matched with a different participant within this game.

Click “Next” to begin.
