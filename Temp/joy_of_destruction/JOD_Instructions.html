Welcome to the Joy of Destruction Game.

In each round, you are randomly paired with another anonymous participant.

  • You and the other participant each start with 10 points.
  • You choose how many of your own points to spend to destroy the other participant’s points.
  • The other participant simultaneously makes the same kind of decision about your points.

For every 1 point you spend on destruction, the other participant loses 1 point. The same applies in the other direction.

Let:

  • a = the number of points you choose to spend to destroy the other’s points,
  • b = the number of points the other chooses to spend to destroy your points.

Then your payoff for the round is:

  • 10 − a − b points.

The other participant’s payoff is also 10 − a − b.

You will play this game for 3 rounds. In each round you are re-matched with a different participant within this game.
